ABOUT UBC


The UBC is a global centre for teaching, learning and research, consistently ranked among the top 20 public universities in the world and recently recognized as North America most international university. Since 1915, our motto, Tuum Est (It is Yours), has been a declaration of our commitment to attracting and supporting those who have the drive to shape a better world. As a result, UBC students, faculty and staff continue to embrace innovation and challenge the status quo, placing us at the forefront of discovery, learning and engagement. UBC encourages bold thinking, curiosity and initiative, so you can realize your greatest potential. Quicklinks.

STUDENT VISA SERVICE

Our Visa Service specialises in securing Tier 4 student visas. SI-UK visa consultants are qualified by the Office of the Immigration Services Commissioner (OISC) and will ensure you receive your visa in time

HEALTH

The Medicine Service covers applications for medicine, dentistry and veterinary studies, some of the toughest courses to successfully enter in the UK

HOSTEL

In partnership with ASIS Group, SI-UK can help your child secure a place at a UBC hostel with our Hostel Service



FINANCIAL AID

Our graduates maintain one of the lowest debt levels when compared with graduates from other, similar research-oriented medical schools.

ACADIMIC ADVISING

Students are assigned a primary academic advising dean who works closely with them throughout medical school to optimize their course of study.

STUDENT WELLNESS

The Office of Medical Student Wellness helps students thrive by providing individual advising, wellness programs and learning environment initiatives.
